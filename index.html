Aim High, Soar High

More than a rhyming mantra, it's a compact philosophy of aspiration and transcendence.

In just four words, it fuses intentionality and momentum, urging students not merely to set lofty goals but to propel themselves beyond them.

The words capture the way our school nurtures curiosity and ambition, shaping students who don't just dream but act on those dreams.

Each time I speak those words, I'm reminded that I belong to an institution recognized as the pride of the southern sky, a place where curiosity is sharpened and ambitions take flight.

We carry our school's light beyond the horizon.
